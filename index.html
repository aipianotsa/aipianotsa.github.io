What is pIAno?

The pIAno is a revolutionary new piano “robot” that is paving the interdisciplinary field of music and artificial intelligence. With the pIAno, you can easily earn how to play your favorite genre/type of music on piano!



How does pIAno work?

1. Play some of your favorite songs on the pIAno.

2. Press the “instruct” me button.

3. The pIAno uses proprietary machine learning algorithms that uses the songs that you played as input in order to create an instructional plan that is unique to you. The plan will incorporate both the type of music you like and help you become more advance at the piano.

4. The pIAno has LED lights at the bottom of each key to show the player which notes to play and when.

5. Whether it’s Bach, Polka or House,we have you covered. pIAno slowly teaches you relevant techniques with increasing complexity and even has instantaneous error correction.

Why should I use pIAno?

Learning piano traditionally has a set, stiff path of learning, with end goal being complex pieces of classical music. This turns off many hopeful musicians to pursuing this instrument. Our product, pIAno, tailors education to YOUR interests. With artificial intelligence, we not only teach you the techniques you want to learn, but we teach it to fit your learning style!

Our proprietary AI algorithm uses GAN’s (Generative Adversarial Networks), a rapidly developing branch of Artificial Intelligence that can create new media based on given inputs. GAN’s are a type of unsupervised learning, which means the network receives just the input and is told to derive some structure or pattern by looking at the relationships between the inputs. With the power of GAN’s, pIAno is able to develop new music similar to the tastes of the user.



What's next for pIAno?

Besides for continuously refining our machine learning algorithm, a future improvement to our product will be an integration with the Khan Academy learning site. Khan Academy is an educational website that uses AI in order to create an educational plan for students in academic areas. This will lead to students advancement in education, but also curiosity driven self-learning, an essential skill for the new age.

Safety & Security

Given popular culture and interpretation of it in movies, many associate Artificial Intelligence with Terminator-like scenarios where machines rise up against humans. However, this is not the case! Our product, pIAno, uses artificial intelligence to help teach you, just as a human piano teacher might. However, pIAno does it in a smarter, quicker, more efficient manner. We put in great efforts to ensure that our robot follows Isaac Asimov’s Three Laws of Robotics and is as safe and secure as possible.

The main potential liability and safety concern of the pIAno is privacy. There is a worry with privacy when it comes to data storage but pIAno uses only the more secure data storage services available. pIAno uses the industry standard of AES-235 when encrypting data.
